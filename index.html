Clear expectations

What works—and what a web app cannot promise.

Will Pacefold update itself?

Yes. Pacefold checks after launch, every 30 minutes while open, when it regains focus and when connectivity returns. A new release activates automatically and reloads once. No manual download is required.

Will notifications work when Pacefold is closed?

No static web app can reliably schedule local notifications after Edge has been fully closed. Keep Pacefold open or minimized. Its taskbar code, favicon and on-screen signals continue to work even when system notifications are blocked.

Does it need administrator permission?

Edge web-app installation normally stays inside your Windows profile, but an employer can disable installation, notifications or taskbar badges through policy. Pacefold does not attempt to bypass those controls.

Where is the activity record stored?

In this browser’s local storage on this device. Pacefold has no account, analytics endpoint or cloud database.
