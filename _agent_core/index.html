Clear expectations

What works—and what a browser cannot promise.

Will Pacefold update itself?

Yes. It checks after launch, periodically while open, when it regains focus and when connectivity returns. Updates wait rather than reloading over setup or an unfinished capture.

Is OneNote sync really silent?

After a one-time Microsoft sign-in and destination choice, captures queue locally and retry in the background. Microsoft Entra registration is required, and university policy may require administrator approval. Read the exact setup.

Does the sound player control Spotify or YouTube?

No. Pacefold generates its own offline textures and can play a local file for the session or a direct HTTPS audio source. It does not pretend it can control unrelated streaming sites.

Will notifications work when Pacefold is closed?

Not reliably. Keep the installed app open or minimized for scheduled JavaScript cues. Taskbar, favicon and in-app signals work even when notifications are blocked.

Can it change my monitor’s colour temperature?

It can change only Pacefold’s own interface. Full-display colour temperature belongs to Windows or monitor software, so Pacefold offers a user-initiated shortcut to Night light.

Does it need administrator permission?

Edge installation normally stays inside the Windows profile, but workplace policy can disable installation, Microsoft consent, notifications, background activity or badges. Pacefold never bypasses those controls.
